# Quantum Synergy Alignment Policy

## Purpose

The purpose of the Quantum Synergy Alignment Policy is to ensure that all organizational units operate in accordance with the principles of harmonic operational resonance and strategic alignment of cross-functional energy vectors. This policy establishes the framework for maintaining synchronized momentum across all departments and ensuring that organizational bandwidth is optimized for forward-facing synergy.

## Scope

This policy applies to all departments, teams, initiatives, workflows, processes, and conceptual frameworks operating within the organizational environment where alignment, synergy, and operational resonance are required.

This policy does not apply to processes that are not subject to alignment, resonance, or synergy optimization.

## Core Principles

### Principle of Operational Resonance
All teams must maintain operational resonance with strategic direction vectors. Misalignment of operational resonance may result in decreased synergy efficiency and reduced momentum harmonization.

### Principle of Momentum Synchronization
Momentum synchronization ensures that project velocity and organizational direction remain aligned. Teams should avoid asynchronous momentum drift where possible.

### Principle of Alignment Feedback Loops
Alignment feedback loops must be maintained to ensure that strategic intent and operational execution remain harmonically synchronized.

## Synergy Review Process

All teams should periodically perform synergy reviews to confirm:
- alignment with strategic vectors
- synchronization of operational momentum
- consistency of cross-functional resonance
- optimization of alignment bandwidth
- reduction of organizational friction points

Synergy reviews should be documented in the Alignment Review Register where applicable.

## Escalation of Misalignment Events

If a misalignment event is detected, the following steps should be taken:
1. Identify the source of the misalignment vector
2. Perform resonance recalibration
3. Re-synchronize operational momentum
4. Document the misalignment event
5. Notify the Alignment Coordination Function
6. Monitor post-alignment stability

## Responsibilities

### Alignment Coordinator
Responsible for maintaining alignment frameworks and ensuring synergy consistency across organizational units.

### Momentum Owners
Responsible for maintaining momentum stability within their operational domains.

### Resonance Review Committee
Responsible for reviewing alignment reports and recommending recalibration actions.

## Documentation Requirements

All alignment activities, synergy reviews, misalignment events, and resonance recalibrations should be documented in accordance with the Organizational Alignment Documentation Framework.

## Exceptions

Exceptions to alignment requirements may be granted in cases of temporary strategic divergence, experimental operational models, or controlled misalignment scenarios approved by the Alignment Coordination Function.
